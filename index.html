What is a Drone?

A Drone is an unmanned aircraft, Here we are specifically talking about Quadcopers which are drones
usually with an X frame with 4 propellers. These drones are controlled using radio transmitters.


There are 3 types of Quadcopters
  • Photography Quadcopters

  • Toy Quadcopters

  • Racing Quadcopters

We will be focusing on photgraphy Quads

Photography Quads are the most epensive type of quadcopters they are used for making movies and videos.
Photography Drones are equipped with a FPV (first person view)system which by WIFI sends the video from
the quad to the screen/phone attached to the controller so you can get you shots right and dont need to to see
the drone to be able to fly it.
